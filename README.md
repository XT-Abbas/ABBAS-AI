## ⚙️ How to Modify

💎 Want to create your own bot? Follow these simple steps:

* ✨ **1. Open `config.js`** ➡ Change the bot name, your number, and settings as you want.
* ✨ **2. Update `thumbnails/` folder** ➡ Replace the default images with your own cute pictures.  
    ⚠ *Must keep the **same file names**!*
* ✨ **3. Run & Enjoy** 🚀  
    ➡ Save everything, deploy, and your custom bot is alive!

> 💡 **Pro Note:** > Do not rename files inside the `thumbnails` folder. Only edit `config.js` for main customization. Now your bot is fully customized with your VIP identity 👑
